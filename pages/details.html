Marvel: Universe of Super Heroes

With key artifacts, interactive installations, infographics, and life-size statues of your favorite Super Heroes, this exhibition illuminates all aspects of Marvel’s eight-decade history: from the iconic characters to the creators who’ve told the stories, from the original comic books to the big-screen blockbusters.

Visitors will enjoy the opportunity to travel through the mysterious mirror dimension of Doctor Strange; get up-close glimpses of concept sketches, props, and costumes from Marvel movies; pose for selfies alongside Black Panther, Spider-Man, and other characters; and even see the original art from some of Marvel’s most notable comics.

Universe Of Super Heroes unfolds as a captivating odyssey into the mystical mirror dimension of Doctor Strange, beckoning visitors to embark on a profound journey through the intricate realms of magic and mysticism. This immersive experience transcends mere visual allure, offering a comprehensive showcase that includes intricate concept sketches, authentic props, and iconic costumes sourced from Marvel's cinematic masterpieces. Within the confines of this exhibition, attendees are treated to a rare glimpse into the dynamic creative process, tracing the evolution of beloved characters from their humble origins in comic books to the spectacular grandeur of the big screen.

Not confined to a passive viewing experience, Marvel: Universe Of Super Heroes invites active participation, allowing attendees to engage intimately with life-size statues embodying the essence of Marvel's legendary characters. Among these larger-than-life figures, the regal Black Panther and the acrobatic Spider-Man stand ready, becoming interactive focal points that beckon visitors to weave their personal narratives through the lens of their cameras. Beyond the visual spectacle, the exhibition delves into the very fabric of Marvel's narrative tapestry, unveiling original art from some of the most pivotal comics in the Marvel universe. In this expansive celebration, Marvel: Universe Of Super Heroes meticulously unravels the rich tapestry of Marvel's eight-decade history, paying profound homage to the visionary creators behind the stories and the key artifacts that have solidified their place as enduring symbols within this extraordinary universe.
