Frequently Asked Questions

Who is the strongest Avenger?

The Hulk, with his incredible strength and resilience, stands out as the mightiest Avenger.

What is Captain America's shield made of?

Captain America's iconic shield is made of vibranium, a rare and powerful fictional metal.

Who is Thor's mischievous adopted brother?

Loki, the god of mischief, is Thor's adopted brother and a frequent adversary of the Avengers.

What organization is Nick Fury the director of?

Nick Fury serves as the director of S.H.I.E.L.D. (Strategic Homeland Intervention, Enforcement, and Logistics Division).

What is Black Widow's real name?

Natasha Romanoff, also known as Black Widow, is a skilled spy and assassin in the Marvel universe.

Who created the Iron Man suit?

ony Stark, a brilliant billionaire and inventor, created the Iron Man suit to fight against threats to humanity.
